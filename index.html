The Competition Pathway: Circle of Glory ‚öΩ

Our football ecosystem is built on growth and challenge, creating a complete cycle of development and top-tier competition.

1.

ELITE LEAGUE

Highest Standard

The pinnacle of the domestic game. It sets the **highest benchmark** for club performance and professionalism in the region.

2.

PROVOST CUP

Foundation Stage

The primary **youth development** platform. It feeds prospective young talent into the feeder leagues leading up to the Elite division.

3.

MIKOKO LEAGUE

Domestic Tier 1

The **largest senior championship**. Clubs here compete for league title and, crucially, qualification to the Glory Champion Cup.

4.

GLORY CHAMPION CUP

Continental Gateway

The **Elite Knockout Cup** where teams from both Mikoko and Elite Leagues battle for the highest cup honor and international placement.

This flow emphasizes that the Elite League drives the standards, the Provost Cup fuels the talent pool, and the Glory Champion Cup is the ultimate contested prize.
